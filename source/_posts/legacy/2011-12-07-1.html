1,添加源：sudo gedit /etc/apt/sources.list

deb http://archive.cloudera.com/debian lucid-cdh3 contrib
deb-src http://archive.cloudera.com/debian lucid-cdh3 contrib


2，安装密钥： curl -s http://archive.cloudera.com/debian/archive.key | sudo apt-key add -

3，更新源：sudo apt-get update
